Tutoring Programs

Starpath Academy is a South African tutoring and academic support academy dedicated to helping learners reach their full potential academically, personally, and spiritually. We provide quality tutoring services designed to strengthen understanding, improve confidence, and prepare learners for future STEM careers.

Our programs are aligned with the CAPS and IEB curricula, ensuring that learners receive the support they need to excel in their studies and develop critical thinking skills for lifelong success.

Our Tutoring Subjects

At Starpath Academy, we offer specialized tutoring in key subjects to help learners build strong foundations and excel in their academic journey. Our tutors are dedicated to providing personalized support that meets each learner's unique needs.

We focus on developing critical thinking, problem-solving, and independent learning skills that prepare learners for STEM-related careers and opportunities.
